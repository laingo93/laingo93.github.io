Online Kettlebell Programming

Our unique kettlebell programming in 6 week blocks of 3 - 4 days a week of workouts.

and alternatives.

Small Group Kettlebell Training

Not your regular kettlebell class. Specifically developed for strength and hypertrophy in 6 week blocks.
Instructor led ensuring proper technique and intensity, this is the best way to get the most out of training.
Includes nutrition planning to suit individual needs in the same way as 1-2-1 personal training would,
as well as excerise tracking through our app.

A free online recorded kettlebell session 3 times a week. Movements will be based around the more regular kettlebell excerises such as
squats, lunges, presses, cleans, snatch, swings, alonside bodyweight excerises to get a good workout in a small space and time

One to One Personal Training

Gym or home based one to one personal training involving a tailored workout plan utilising mainly kettlebells.
